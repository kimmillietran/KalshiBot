# PR-11.2 — Feature Duplication Consolidation

## Summary

Consolidates truly identical feature computations identified in the pre-M11 audit and documents intentionally distinct semantics for momentum and volatility.

## Consolidated (identical formulas)

| Duplicate | Shared helper |
|-----------|---------------|
| `midProbability` in 5 research parsers | `midProbabilityFromCents` (`src/lib/features/contractPricing.ts`) |
| `spreadSidePercent` in market features, trading guards, regime parser | `spreadSidePercent` / `maxSpreadSidePercent` |
| `TIME_REMAINING_BUCKET_DEFINITIONS` in decision trace attribution | Re-export from `dimensions/bucketDefinitions.ts` |
| `mapCandles` in mispricing + vol-premium parsers | `mapEvaluationCandleSnapshots` |
| Local percentile/mean helpers | `src/lib/utils/stats.ts` (`quantile`, `percentile`, `averageFinite`) |

## Intentionally separate (documented in `FEATURE_SEMANTICS.md`)

| Feature | Trading | Research |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Momentum | `recentMomentum` (5-bar feature vector) | `computeBtcMomentumPct` / `computeResearchObservationMomentumPercent` |
| Volatility | `rollingVolatility` (price std dev) | `estimateRealizedVolatility` (log-return annualized) |
