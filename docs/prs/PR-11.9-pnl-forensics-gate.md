# PR 11.9 — In-Sample PnL Forensics Gate

## Why this exists before full M12

M11.6 replay showed positive net in-sample PnL across four hypotheses and 1,163 filled step-level trades with zero cost/fillability kills. That is encouraging, but step-level trades are **not independent** — repeated entries in the same market/day can inflate apparent edge.

Before investing in the full M12 Trade-PnL OOS Overlay, this milestone asks:

```text
Where did the M11.6 positive in-sample PnL come from?
```

It is a cheap kill-switch: broad, credible-looking PnL can proceed; concentrated PnL should pause.

## How PnL concentration is measured

The gate re-derives filled trades from the same M11.6 replay inputs (without changing M11.6 calculations) and aggregates at three levels:

| Level | Rule |
|-------|------|
| Step-level | Descriptive turnover only (filled trade count) |
| Market-level | Sum all repeated entries in the same market |
| Day-level | Sum all markets on the same UTC calendar day |

Decompositions include side, day equity curve, month, market, hypothesis, and optional regime tags.

Default family proceed thresholds (`DEFAULT_PNL_FORENSICS_GATE_CONFIG`):

- Top day ≤ 50% of positive PnL
- Top 3 days ≤ 80% of positive PnL
- Top market ≤ 40% of total PnL
- No single side ≥ 90% of positive PnL
- ≥ 2 calendar months with positive PnL
- ≥ 5 unique trading days with positive PnL
- Market-level aggregation remains positive

Thresholds are not tuned to force a pass on tiny samples.

## Why step-level trades are not independent

Each replay step in a market can generate another filled trade. A single favorable market move can produce many correlated wins. Concentration verdicts prefer **market/day aggregation** over raw step counts. No statistical significance tests are run on step-level trades.

## How to interpret proceed vs pause

| Family verdict | Meaning |
|----------------|---------|
| `proceed-to-trade-pnl-oos` | Concentration checks passed; worth running full M12 OOS PnL experiment |
| `pause-family-concentrated-pnl` | PnL likely driven by side/day/month/market/repeated-entry concentration |
| `collect-more-data` | Positive but too few months/days to judge breadth |
| `insufficient-data` | No filled trades or replay artifact unusable |

A **pass does not validate a strategy**. It only clears the family for the next OOS PnL experiment.

## What this does not prove

- Not out-of-sample evidence
- Not alpha or statistical significance
- No queue position, latency, partial fills, or adverse selection modeling
- Does not change hypothesis generation, validation, replay, or promotion

## Connection to M12 Trade-PnL OOS Overlay

M11.9 is the forensic gate immediately before M12. M12 should run only when `recommendFullM12: true` (family verdict `proceed-to-trade-pnl-oos`). Otherwise the family should widen data, reduce concentration, or pause.
