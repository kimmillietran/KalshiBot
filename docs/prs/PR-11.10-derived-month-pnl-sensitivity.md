# PR 11.10 — Dec 2025 / Derived-Month PnL Sensitivity

## Why this exists after M11.9

M11.9 found that calibration-fade family in-sample PnL is positive but **month-concentrated**:

- Family net PnL was dominated by calendar month `2025-12`
- Day and market concentration were comparatively broad
- Recommended next action: `rerun-excluding-derived-month`

M11.10 answers the follow-up question:

```text
Does calibration-fade still have positive in-sample PnL if 2025-12 is excluded?
```

This milestone decides whether full **M12 Trade-PnL OOS Overlay** is still worth building.

## Why 2025-12 is treated as sensitive

December 2025 is a known **derived-settlement-sensitive** period in this research corpus. Markets in that month may use `derived expiration_value` settlements rather than official settlement inputs. M11.9 flagged month concentration in this period as the primary pause driver even when day/market concentration looked broad.

M11.10 treats `2025-12` as the default sensitive month (`--sensitive-month` / `--exclude-month`).

## What this does not prove

- This does **not** prove or disprove alpha
- This is **not** out-of-sample validation
- Excluding a month is a diagnostic filter, not a live trading rule
- A proceed recommendation only means full M12 may be worth building next

## Variants computed

| Variant | Filter |
|---------|--------|
| `full-corpus` | All re-derived filled trades |
| `excluding-sensitive-month` | Exclude `calendarMonth=2025-12` |
| `sensitive-month-only` | Only `calendarMonth=2025-12` |
| `official-only` | Exclude derived-settlement markets when keys are discoverable; otherwise exclude `2025-12` (heuristic) |
| `derived-only` | Only derived-settlement markets when keys are discoverable; otherwise only `2025-12` (heuristic) |

Each variant reports family-level and hypothesis-level PnL forensics metrics plus deltas versus full corpus (retention shares, sign flips).

## How to interpret full vs excluding-2025-12

| Observation | Interpretation |
|-------------|----------------|
| Excluding month stays net positive with broad remaining months | PnL is not solely an artifact of Dec 2025 |
| Excluding month collapses to ≤ 0 | Family PnL is materially dependent on the sensitive month |
| High retention but sparse days/months | Positive but underpowered — collect more official months |
| Most hypotheses sign-flip negative after exclusion | Edge may be hypothesis-specific to the sensitive month |

Compare `netPnlRetentionShare`, `hypothesisSignFlips`, and `topMonthShare` on the excluding-month variant.

## When to proceed to full M12

Family recommendation `proceed-to-trade-pnl-oos` when:

- Excluding `2025-12` remains net positive
- At least 2 non-sensitive months contribute positive PnL
- No single remaining month dominates > 60% of PnL
- Unique trading day count remains sufficient (≥ 5 by default)

`recommendFullM12: true` only when the family recommendation is `proceed-to-trade-pnl-oos`.

## When to pause or pivot

| Recommendation | Meaning |
|----------------|---------|
| `pause-family-derived-month-dependent` | Positive PnL appears dependent on the sensitive month |
| `reject-family-derived-month-artifact` | Excluding month removes positive PnL or most hypotheses flip negative |
| `collect-more-official-months` | Excluding month is positive but sparse/underpowered |
| `insufficient-data` | Not enough filled trades to judge |

Thresholds are not tuned to force a proceed verdict.
